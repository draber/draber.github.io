What is a bookmarklet?

Bookmarklets are small pieces of code that can enhance the functionality of a website. They reside, just like regular bookmarks, in your browser‚Äôs bookmark menu. Unfortunately, there is no straightforward way to assign an icon to them.

Does it work everywhere?

Spelling Bee Assistant works only with modern browsers and has been built with laptops or desktop computers in mind. Installing bookmarklets on a phone or a tablet is a bit complex but can be done according to Google. There is a good chance it will work on larger mobile devices but this has not been tested. It can't be installed on the NYT app though.

Does it not spoil the game?

Other people have been creating tools with a similar purpose which - according to this article - the Spelling Bee community seems to like well.

Use the Assistant sparsely, don't launch it before having solved a good chunk of the game on your own. Don't open the Spoilers panel before you really need to. Only hit the magic button when you eventually run completely out of ideas. At least for me it works well this way.
